Player Stats
============
- hunger: Hunger level (0-100).
- rest: Rest/energy level (0-100).
- stress: Stress level (0-100).
- comfort: Comfort level (0-100).
- baseMinComfortTemp: Base minimum comfortable temperature.
- baseMaxComfortTemp: Base maximum comfortable temperature.
- head_efficiency: Efficiency of the head (0-100).
- torso_efficiency: Efficiency of the torso (0-100).
- limbs_efficiency: Efficiency of the limbs (0-100).
- inventory_capacity: Number of inventory slots.
- max_weight: Maximum carry weight.
- water: Ship water level (0-100).
- fuel: Ship fuel level (0-100).
- morale: AI morale, flees if low (0-100).

Weapon Stats
============
- damageType: The type of damage (e.g., kinetic, energy).
- damageAmount: The base damage amount.
- penetration: Affects enemy armour.
- accuracy: The base hit chance (0-100).
- range: The effective range in tiles.
- comfortPenalty: Penalty to comfort when fired.

Armour Stats
============
- durability: Current durability of the armour.
- maxDurability: Maximum durability of the armour.
- kinetic_resistance: Resistance to kinetic damage (0-100).
- energy_resistance: Resistance to energy damage (0-100).
- toxin_resistance: Resistance to toxin damage (0-100).
- radiation_resistance: Resistance to radiation damage (0-100).
- tempMax: Increases maximum comfortable temperature.
- tempMin: Decreases minimum comfortable temperature.

Combat Stats
============
- movementPerTurn: Tiles the entity can move per turn.
- initiativeRoll: Determines turn order.
- stunned: Status effect, skips next turn.
- bleeding: Status effect, damage over time.
- infected: Status effect, damage over time.
- AIM_BONUS: Accuracy bonus per aim stack.
- MAX_AIM_STACKS: Maximum number of aim stacks.
- STRESS_OPTIMAL_MIN: Lower bound for optimal stress accuracy bonus.
- STRESS_OPTIMAL_MAX: Upper bound for optimal stress accuracy bonus.
- STRESS_PENALTY_1_MIN: Lower bound for first level of stress accuracy penalty.
- STRESS_PENALTY_1_MAX: Upper bound for first level of stress accuracy penalty.
- STRESS_PENALTY_2_MIN: Lower bound for second level of stress accuracy penalty.
- STRESS_PENALTY_2_MAX: Upper bound for second level of stress accuracy penalty.
- HEAD_ACCURACY_PENALTY: Accuracy penalty if head is damaged.
- TORSO_ACCURACY_PENALTY: Accuracy penalty if torso is damaged.
- LIMBS_MOVEMENT_THRESHOLD: Threshold for movement penalty when limbs are damaged.
- BASE_DODGE: Base dodge chance.
- UNARMED_DAMAGE: Damage when unarmed.
- UNARMED_ACCURACY: Accuracy when unarmed.
- BLEEDING_DAMAGE_PER_TURN: Damage per turn from bleeding.
- TORSO_BLEEDING_THRESHOLD: Torso health percentage to trigger bleeding.
- INFECTED_DAMAGE_PER_TURN: Damage per turn from infection.
- INFECTED_DURATION: Duration of infection in turns.
- FLEE_MORALE_THRESHOLD: Morale threshold for AI to flee.
- FIRST_STRIKE_BONUS: Accuracy bonus for initiating combat.
- OUT_OF_RANGE_PENALTY: Accuracy penalty per tile beyond weapon range.
